Mold Assessor

Field notes, experiments, and lessons learned as I level up from hands-on remediation to formal assessment. This page collects my articles and updates as I prepare for the CIE exam in October 2025 and keep pushing into the science of buildings and bioaerosols.

Where I am right now

I have four years in the trenches of mold remediation and water loss work. I am shifting focus to assessment: better diagnostics, cleaner scopes, and documentation that stands up to scrutiny. My near target is the Certified Indoor Environmentalist (CIE) in October 2025. From there, I plan to deepen into microbiology and building science while keeping my reports practical for homeowners, property managers, and contractors.
