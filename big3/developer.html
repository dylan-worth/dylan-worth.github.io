What I’m Building

**SMB Assessments CRM (work‑in‑progress):** a focused, offline‑capable client + jobs tracker designed for restoration and assessment workflows.

  • Offline‑first: Works in the field; sync later. Local JSON storage with export/import for portability.
  • Clean documentation: Clients, sites, rooms, moisture logs, and photos mapped to a single job timeline.
  • No vendor lock‑in: Your data stays yours; simple schemas and easy backups.

Roadmap includes permissions, printable scopes, and basic analytics. (No public repo yet; still shaping the data model.)
