Repair Troubleshooting

A/C is not blowing cold air?

Check your filter. If it's dirty, replace it. Turn the unit OFF for 8 hours to let the coils defrost. If it still doesn't cool, contact the office.

No hot water?

Check the electrical breaker panel. Reset the hot water breaker, wait one hour, and check again.

Well water pump stopped working?

Locate the GFI outlet near the pump. Press the 'RESET' button (usually the top button in the center). This often trips after Florida storms.
