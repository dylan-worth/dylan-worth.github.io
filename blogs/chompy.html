The Chompy Slayer

Okay, so in my Runescape life span, I was a completionist with that trimmed cape-ability. One of the requirements to achieve such status was getting 4,000 chompy kills. Not only did it satisfy the requirement, but it also came with that fashion-scape Expert Dragon Archer Hat. However, in my opinion, the black Dragon Archer Hat looked way better than the gold Expert version.

So, after achieving 1,000,000 arrow shafts on my SP account, I decided to start the chompy mission. However, I got burnt out on Runescape at the time, so I went on a hiatus. When I got back into the groove, I decided I would achieve this task on an Ultimate Ironman on the live server, so I created Ultimate Iron(can). I didn’t even last the day playing it since I clearly wasn’t ready to commit to the game again.

However, a week or two ago (sometime in August 2024), I decided to make a new Ironman, but this time without the restrictions. I chose regular Ironman mode, so I had restrictions in terms of not playing with others, but this was only to keep the game fair.

A player named Ann asked when I was going to start making progress. I needed to get the requirements for the Big Chompy Bird Hunting quest, but I also waited for my wife to go out of town so I could spend some time recording the quest and my first hat. I really don’t want to make this a static article; I want it to become a dynamic progress log of how I’m doing during this challenge.

By the end of today, I’ll probably have 125 chompy kills. I currently have 95, but I’m about to log in and continue the grind for my sixth hat. At the bottom of this page, there will be a super fancy table with a crossed-out list of what I’ve achieved so far.

Ending the day off with 125 chompy kills and plan to get a few more tomorrow. It'll progress over time so keep checking back if you are interested.
