Replacement Apps/Software

While these apps may have to be used from time to time the goal is to replace all the apps with ones that I can replace. The others will come in do time and pretty quickly when I have to figure stuff out with grapheneOS. Under, I plan on listing the common apps I plan on replacing and I hope it can help you.

Proton Suite: I wanted to discuss this one first as it will replace quite a bit of my every day applications. Disclaimer: I pay $11.99/month for their premium service.
Proton Mail: It is an end to end encrypted email service.
Proton Pass: It is a password manager that is encrypted.
Proton Calendar: It is an encrypted calendar.
Proton Mail: It is an encrypted drive, it has a photos section, and it has a doc editor.
Proton VPN: End to end encrypted where they can't see your activity of what you are doing on the VPN.

Open Street Maps: This is a free and open-source app where you download the area that you want to map out. For example, I downloaded my entire state, so I'm able to use it on the streets app and use it offline as well.

Ente Photos: This is a free and open-source app that allows me to have my 'Google Photos' vibe with memories. You can get 5GB of free storage, but I went ahead and upgraded to $2.99/month for now, until I get my NAS and Nextcloud set up. It's a gorgeous application that is also end-to-end encrypted!

Revolt: If you have used Discord for community chats while gaming, then Revolt will excite you. This is a Discord look-alike that is free and open-source. You can host your own server or create a community in the official servers.
