Rockefeller

Architects of the American Century

I. The Flexner Report & Medical Monopoly

In 1910, the Rockefeller Foundation, alongside Carnegie, funded the Flexner Report. This document was the "hatchet" used to dismantle holistic, herbal, and homeopathic medical schools.

"The patient is the target; the drug is the bullet." - Rockefeller Medicine Principle.

By linking funding exclusively to schools that taught petrochemical-based pharmaceuticals, the family created the modern medical-industrial complex. Natural cures were rebranded as "quackery" to ensure a captured market for Standard Oil's chemical offshoots.

II. Jekyll Island & The Debt Trap

In November 1910, Rockefeller representatives (including Senator Nelson Aldrich, whose daughter married John D. Rockefeller Jr.) met secretly at Jekyll Island to draft the Federal Reserve Act.

This move shifted the US from a sovereign currency to a private debt-based system. Through Chase National Bank, the family secured a permanent seat at the head of the global financial table, profiting from the interest on the very money the government prints.

III. General Education Board: Molding Workers

Established in 1902 with $180 million, the **General Education Board** (GEB) was not designed to foster critical thinking. Its purpose was the mass production of a compliant labor force.

"In our dreams, people yield themselves with perfect docility to our molding hands... We shall not try to make these people into philosophers or men of learning."
— Frederick T. Gates, Advisor to Rockefeller (1913)

The school system was redesigned to mirror the factory: bells, rows, obedience to authority, and standardized testing. "Thinkers" were seen as a threat to the stability of the industrial hierarchy.
