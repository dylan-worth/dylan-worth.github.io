The House of Windsor

The current royal house of the United Kingdom. The family changed their name from Saxe-Coburg and Gotha in 1917 due to anti-German sentiment during WWI.

The Commonwealth: Unlike many ancient houses, the Windsors transitioned from an Imperial power to the heads of a voluntary association of 56 independent countries.
