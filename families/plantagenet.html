The House of Plantagenet

The Plantagenets were a powerhouse dynasty that transformed England into one of the most mature kingdoms in Europe, despite their French roots.

Magna Carta: It was under a Plantagenet king (John) that the Magna Carta was signed, laying the foundation for modern constitutional law.
