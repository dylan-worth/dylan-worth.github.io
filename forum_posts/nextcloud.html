Nextcloud - Cloud Storage Upgraded

Dylan Worth March 23, 2024

We use apps to store our data, and share memories with friends. However, many large companies take advantage and sell your personal data. It may not seem like a big deal but we should choose who we share our data with. If you are looking for a drive alternative to Google, Microsoft or other big companies. Let's talk about Nextcloud. Nextcloud is a FOSS cloud service that you can run on a local PC.

Personally, there are some things online that I don't mind sharing with the world. And, other things that I just want my family to see. However, using cloud services like Google, One Drive, etc.., store all my personal data on their servers. Google recently had an issue with losing 3 months of stored data on someone's drive. That is absolute bonkers.

Once you've got Nextcloud up and running, the possibilities are endless! I highly recommend setting it up on a spare PC or Raspberry Pi for full control. With Nextcloud, you can install various 'apps' to enhance your experience, such as a music app that's like having your own Spotify or YouTube Music, Collabra for all your office needs, and Memories to cherish your precious moments. Not sure where to get started? Check out the Nextcloud website for guidance on setting up your very own Nextcloud server. Or, if you need a video guide on setting this up with Linode check out Techhut on Youtube.

Some of the features that you can get installed on your Nextcloud I mentioned above. I just wanted to go over a few with everyone so they have an idea of what using your own cloud service can do for you.

Music

As I quickly mentioned above, this app allows you to play your mp3 files from your cloud service using any device you connect to the cloud. It mimics that Youtube Music app which allows you to make playlist, randomize choices, or have your top picks.



▸ Music for Owncloud is an app that allows users to stream their music collection directly from their Owncloud server.

▸ It provides a simple and intuitive interface for browsing and playing music files stored in Owncloud.

▸ Music for Owncloud supports various audio formats, including MP3, FLAC, OGG, and more.

▸ Users can create playlists, shuffle tracks, and control playback using the web interface or dedicated mobile apps.

▸ It also includes features like album artwork display, metadata support, and search functionality for easy navigation.

▸ Music for Owncloud enhances the Owncloud experience by adding a convenient way to manage and listen to your music library.



Music for Owncloud GitHub Repository

Cons:

▸ Limited Features: The Music app for Owncloud with Nextcloud may have limited features compared to dedicated music streaming services. It may lack advanced features like personalized recommendations, curated playlists, or integration with external music libraries.

▸ Dependency on Self-Hosting: Using the Music app requires hosting your own Nextcloud instance, which may require technical expertise and ongoing maintenance. This can be a barrier for users who prefer a simpler, hosted solution for managing their music collection.

▸ Performance and Scalability: Depending on the resources available on your server and the number of users accessing the Music app, performance and scalability may be a concern. Large music collections or high traffic can impact the responsiveness of the application.

▸ Compatibility Issues: The Music app may encounter compatibility issues with certain browsers, devices, or audio file formats. This can result in playback issues or limitations in accessing your music collection across different platforms.

▸ Limited Integration: The Music app may have limited integration with external services or devices compared to mainstream music streaming platforms. This can limit your ability to access your music collection across different devices or share playlists with friends.

▸ Lack of Offline Playback: Unlike some dedicated music streaming services, the Music app may not offer offline playback capabilities. This means you may need an internet connection to access your music collection, which can be inconvenient in certain situations.



Collabra

▸ Collabora Online is a collaborative office suite integrated with Nextcloud.

▸ It allows users to create, edit, and collaborate on documents, spreadsheets, and presentations in real-time.

▸ Collabora Online supports popular file formats like DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, ODF, and more.

▸ Users can work together on documents simultaneously, with changes synced in real-time.

▸ It provides features like track changes, commenting, and version history for efficient collaboration.

▸ Collabora Online enhances productivity by enabling teams to collaborate seamlessly within Nextcloud.



Collabora Online for Nextcloud GitHub Repository

Collabra is an office suite similar to Microsoft Office, it has the typical spreadsheet, word processor, slideshow creator, and more. What is neat, you are able to access this from anywhere you have Nextcloud installed.

Memories

Memories is a Photo viewer app a lot like Google Photos. You can store as much photos as you have memory on your server. You have memories, albums, face recognition, and more.

▸ Memories is a feature in Nextcloud that organizes photos and videos based on when and where they were taken.

▸ It automatically groups media files into albums or collections, making it easier to reminisce about past events.

▸ Memories provides a visually appealing way to browse and share personal memories with friends and family.

▸ It uses intelligent algorithms to categorize and organize media files, creating a seamless user experience.

▸ Users can navigate through their memories, relive past moments, and share them with others directly from Nextcloud.

▸ Memories enhances the overall user experience by providing a convenient and organized way to interact with personal media collections.

▸ Limited availability of features compared to mainstream cloud storage services.

▸ Potential compatibility issues with certain devices or operating systems.

▸ Dependency on self-hosting Nextcloud, which may require technical expertise and maintenance.

▸ Limited integration with external services or platforms.

▸ Relatively smaller user base compared to established cloud storage providers.



In Conclusion:

Nextcloud is awesome because it puts you in charge of your digital life. You get to decide how you store your files, share them with friends, and collaborate on projects—all without sacrificing your privacy. It's like having your own personal cloud that you can customize to fit your style and needs. Whether you're a student juggling schoolwork, a gamer sharing files with friends, a young professional managing projects, or a family keeping memories safe and accessible, Nextcloud gives you the tools to stay organized and in control of your digital world.
