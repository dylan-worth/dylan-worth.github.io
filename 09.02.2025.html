My Daughter Turns 7 Today!

Seven years ago I became a dad for the very first time. My oldest daughter, the one who made me a father, is celebrating her 7th birthday today.

She is the leader of the pack, oldest of four kids, and she has a personality that fills the whole room. She loves Stitch (yes, she has watched Lilo & Stitch more times than I can count and can quote him perfectly). She enjoys playing Roblox (supervised of course) and she is already turning into quite the artist. For her age I think she has real talent, her sketches and doodles surprise me all the time.

She is also completely in love with music. Whether she is singing, dancing, or just filling the house with her favorite songs, there is always a rhythm following her around.

I am proud of her not only because she is creative, fun, and bright, but because she was the one who started this whole adventure for me as a dad.
