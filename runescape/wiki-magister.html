Description

The Magister (Oreb) is a high-level Slayer boss introduced in the Slayer 120 Expansion (Revision 881). He is a corrupted necromancer found deep within the Sophanem Slayer Dungeon.

He is the first boss to require a specific Slayer level to kill (115), making him an exclusive encounter for dedicated players.

Mechanics

Accessing the Magister requires a Key to the Crossing, which is dropped by Soul Devourers and Corrupted Creatures. Each fight consumes one key.

The fight revolves around managing his "Corruption" stacks. As the fight progresses, the player takes increased damage but deals increased damage in return.
