The Adventurer's Guide

Welcome to the official Open881 Wiki. This project is a WebGL-based tribute to classic MMOs. Here we document every mechanic, item, and quest currently implemented in the game engine.

Combat System

Combat in Open881 is real-time and based on your character's stats. When you attack, we trigger a swing animation and calculate hit-splats based on your Attack level vs. the enemy's defense.

Splat Color Meaning Logic
Red Successful Hit Damage is dealt to the enemy's HP.
Blue Miss / Block Zero damage dealt. Accuracy roll failed.

Weapons & Equipment

Weapons determine your maximum hit and your attack speed. You can dual-wield a main-hand weapon and a shield.

Skill Mastery

Training skills unlocks new areas and better equipment. Currently, we have the following skills active:

  • Woodcutting: Chop trees for Logs. Higher levels allow Oaking.
  • Attack: Increases your chance to land a successful hit.
  • Strength: Increases the maximum damage you can deal.
  • Hitpoints: Determines how much damage you can take before death.

The World: Lumbridge

Lumbridge is the starting capital of Open881. It features a castle, a church, and the dangerous goblin-infested forests. Keep an eye on the time—as night falls, the street lanterns will light up automatically to guide your path.
