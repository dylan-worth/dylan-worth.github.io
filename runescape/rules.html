Open881 Official Rules

Welcome to Open881. To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone testing this revision, strict adherence to the following rules is required. Ignorance of these rules is not an excuse.

1. Macroing & Third-Party Software The use of bots, auto-clickers, or any third-party software that interacts with the game client to automate gameplay is strictly prohibited. This includes software that generates inputs or reads client data to gain an unfair advantage.
2. Real World Trading (RWT) Buying or selling Open881 items, gold, or accounts for real-world currency is a permanent ban offense for both the buyer and the seller. Donations should only be made through the official store to support server costs.
3. Bug Abuse Knowingly exploiting a bug or glitch to gain an advantage is forbidden. If you discover a bug (e.g., item duplication, safe-spotting unintentional mechanics), you must report it on the forums immediately. Do not share it with other players.
4. Staff Impersonation Pretending to be a Moderator or Administrator to scam or mislead other players will result in an immediate ban. Staff members will never ask for your password.
5. Harassment & Offensive Language While we support free speech, targeted harassment, racism, or hate speech will not be tolerated. Keep the public chat civil.
