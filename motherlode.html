📜 The Lore of Motherlode Mine

Deep beneath the great city of Falador lies the Dwarven Mine, a vast network of tunnels carved by the industrious dwarves of old. Within this labyrinth, the eccentric dwarf Prospector Percy discovered a revolutionary mining technique that would change mining forever.

Percy's method involves mining pay-dirt - a special ore-bearing gravel found in the cave walls. This pay-dirt is then washed through an ingenious water-powered system using a hopper and sluice mechanism. The rushing water separates the valuable ores from the worthless rock, depositing clean ores into collection sacks.

Miners who prove their worth can earn Golden Nuggets - rare treasures hidden within the pay-dirt. These nuggets can be traded with Percy for the legendary Prospector Outfit, which magically enhances one's mining abilities, as well as useful tools like the Coal Bag and Gem Bag.

The mine is not without its dangers - rockfalls frequently block passages, and the ancient water wheel struts require constant maintenance. Experienced miners (level 72+) may gain access to the upper level, where richer ore veins await those brave enough to climb.
