📜 The Motherlode Mine

🏔️ History

Deep beneath Falador lies the ancient Dwarven Mine, a vast network of tunnels carved centuries ago by the skilled dwarves of Keldagrim. Within these depths, the eccentric prospector Percy discovered a revolutionary mining method that would change Gielinor forever.

⚙️ The Pay-dirt System

Percy's ingenious invention uses water-powered sluices to separate valuable ores from worthless rock. Miners extract "pay-dirt" from ore veins, load it into carts, and transport it to the hopper. The water wheel powers the separation process, depositing clean ores into collection sacks.

🪙 Golden Nuggets

Rarely, miners discover golden nuggets hidden within the pay-dirt - precious treasures that can be traded with Percy for special rewards:

  • Prospector Outfit - Magical mining gear (+2.5% XP per piece)
  • Coal Bag - Holds 27 additional coal
  • Gem Bag - Stores up to 60 uncut gems
  • Upper Level Access - Reach richer ore veins

⚠️ Hazards

Rockfalls: Unstable tunnels frequently collapse, blocking passages. Clear them to proceed!

Broken Struts: The water wheel's wooden supports sometimes break. Repair them quickly or the hopper stops working!
