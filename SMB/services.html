Our Specialized Services

We provide the data you need to make informed decisions about your property.

Final Compliance

PRV Inspections

Post-Remediation Verification (PRV) is the most critical step in any restoration project. We act as an independent third party to ensure that the remediation contractor has successfully returned your property to a "Condition 1" (normal fungal ecology) status.

  • Visual inspections for dust and debris
  • Moisture content verification
  • Final air and surface sampling
Advanced Technology

Moisture Mapping

We don't just find water; we map its extent. Using high-resolution infrared thermography, we identify the exact boundaries of water intrusion without ever touching your walls.

  • Identification of hidden pipe leaks
  • Evaluation of building envelope failures
  • Non-invasive diagnostic reporting
Health & Safety

Comprehensive Home Testing

We test for more than just mold. From VOCs to specific allergens, we provide a full profile of your indoor air quality to help you breathe easier.

  • Allergen and particulate screening
  • VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) testing
  • Certified laboratory analysis
