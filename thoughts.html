Fallen World

The future is very uncertain in terms of how it will shape up with the economy, the election, and the seemingly ever-increasing prices of houses. I worry for my children and what they will have to endure throughout their lives, as the world seems to be getting worse. I hope this isn't some apocalyptic time we're living through, as that thought is frightening in itself. Just a small ramble of concern for this fallen world, but hopefully, the next generation can find a way to lift it back up.
