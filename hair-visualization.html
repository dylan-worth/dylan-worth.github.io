Size Comparison

A single strand of human hair is approximately 100 microns wide.

In comparison, mold spores are microscopic, typically ranging from 2 to 20 microns.

This means you could fit roughly 5 to 50 mold spores across the width of the single hair strand shown here.

Reference: Typical human hair width ranges from 17 to 181 µm.
