About the Project

Lost City is an open-source RuneScape recreation project led by Pazaz and a team of talented developers. Their goal is to preserve and recreate the magic of RuneScape's iconic eras. Two of the biggest projects under the Lost City umbrella are 2004scape, which captures the nostalgia of May 2004, and 2019scape, recreating the features of RuneScape as they were in 2019.
